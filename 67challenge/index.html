Privacy Policy for 6-7 Challenge

Effective Date: May 28, 2026

Thank you for using 6-7 Challenge. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Summary

6-7 Challenge does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Everything stays on your device. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no third-party trackers.

Information the App Uses

Camera

The app requires access to your camera in order to detect your body movements and count repetitions during the challenge. Camera data is processed in real time, on your device, using Apple's Vision framework. The camera feed is never sent, uploaded, or stored anywhere outside the app — unless you choose to record a video.

Microphone

If you enable video recording, the app uses your device's microphone to record audio together with the video. Audio is only captured during recording sessions you initiate. Audio is never sent off your device.

Photo Library

If you enable video recording, the recorded video is saved to your device's Photos app. The app requests permission only to add new videos. It does not read, browse, or modify any other photos or videos in your library.

Player Name

You may optionally enter a name for the leaderboard. This name is stored only on your device and is not shared with anyone.

Leaderboard and Scores

Your scores are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. They are not synced, uploaded, or shared. If you delete the app, your leaderboard is deleted with it.

Information the App Does NOT Use

  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or any analytics data.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.
  • We do not have a server or backend, so no data is sent to us at any point.
  • We do not share any data with third parties because we do not have any data to share.

Children's Privacy

6-7 Challenge is designed to be safe for all ages, including children. Since the app does not collect or transmit any data, it is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations such as COPPA and GDPR-K. Parents can use the app with full confidence that their child's information stays private.

Permissions You Control

All sensitive permissions (Camera, Microphone, Photo Library) are requested only when needed and can be revoked at any time through your iPhone's Settings:

Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera / Microphone / Photos

The app will continue to work for everything that does not require the revoked permission.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you are always in full control:

  • To delete a single leaderboard entry: long-press the entry in the leaderboard and tap Delete.
  • To clear an entire leaderboard: tap the trash icon next to the leaderboard title.
  • To delete all app data: uninstall the app from your iPhone.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, this policy will be updated and the effective date above will change. The latest version will always be available at the same URL where you originally read it.
